Space Station 14 is a multiplayer game released in 2021, which is a sequel to the legendary and well-known Space Station 13. The game's premise is that you have arrived at the station while working for the NanoTrasen Corporation. It tells the story of how a routine shift at the space station goes awry. Immerse yourself in your role, work with detailed systems, and survive the chaos of this multiplayer role-playing game.

ROLES

  • Captain
  • The head of the entire station.
  • He gives orders, and sometimes they're good.
  • He often realizes that his very important item has been stolen.
  • Often paranoid and armed.
  • Head of Staff
  • Right-hand man and deputy captain.
  • The main role is to manage the service department and change the crew member's access ID, if necessary.
  • He also monitors the work of the station and departments.
  • He often neglects his work and goes out to catch criminals.
  • Head of the Security Service
  • The head of the order at this station.
  • Either good or dead. There's no other option.
  • He goes to sleep and never wakes up.
